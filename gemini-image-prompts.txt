GLOBAL PREFIX (use with every image)

Harmony digital atelier — global style: Cinematic, ultra-refined luxury creative agency aesthetic. Deep charcoal and black surfaces, subtle warm gold accents (not yellow plastic), soft controlled lighting, high dynamic range, minimal composition, architectural precision, editorial fashion-magazine quality. Photorealistic where noted; no text, no logos, no watermarks, no UI chrome unless the prompt explicitly asks for a screen. Clean negative space; works well when desaturated to grayscale in post.
